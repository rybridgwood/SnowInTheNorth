Our Mission

Our mission is to nurture and enrich our students' natural delight in learning and in the powers of mind and body.

Each child's awakening into confident young adulthood requires guided engagement with vital ideas and events of the past to realize fresh possibilities and discoveries for the future.  We aspire to foster in our students those qualities of character consonant with the ideals of a democratic society. We are committed to reaching beyond routine expectations in a learning environment that encourages inquiry and builds its daily practice upon respect for oneself and others.

Arts

Over 50% of North Shore Students Participate in the Fine & Performing Arts. It includes Bands, Clubs, Madrigal Singers, Viking Masquers, Ensembles, Orchestras, and Much More!

Athletics

Over 70% of North Shore Students Participate in Interscholastic Athletics. It includes Baseball, Basketball, Cheerleading, Cross Country & Track, Field Hockey, Football, Gymnastics, Lacrosse, Soccer, Softball, Wrestling and Much More!

Board of Education

The School Board Consists of Seven Individuals Elected By North Shore Community. Their Primary Responsibility is to Provide a School System that Establishes General Policies Consistent with The Laws of NY State and the Rules & Regulations of the State Education Department.

ADA Compliance Notice
